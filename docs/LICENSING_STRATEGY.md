# Licensing strategy

Both reviewed source repositories declare GNU GPL version 3. N1 copies no code,
assets, binaries, schemas, or frontend work from them. The clean-start
foundation is licensed under Apache-2.0, while development review provenance is
kept in notices.

Selected staged-hybrid packaging must treat a bundled or modified GPL engine as
a separately tracked distribution component. Distribution may trigger source,
license, copyright, installation-information, and modification-notice
obligations. Network/process separation alone is not a guarantee that a combined
distribution avoids GPL consequences.

Before shipping engine binaries or adapting substantial code, obtain qualified
legal review of the packaging, linking/IPC design, container distribution,
corresponding-source delivery, written offers, trademark use, and obligations
of every transitive dependency. Do not promise a proprietary distribution model
until that review is complete.
